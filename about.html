About the Authors

Kayla Gray

Hi! My name is Kayla Gray and I am finishing up my last semester of my senior year at SUNY Oswego. I am double-majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics. Apart from taking classes, I currently TA for two computer science courses: CSC212 Principles of Programming and CSC344 Programming Languages. I also am Vice President of Women in Computing and Math Club at SUNY Oswego. As a part of the honors program, I have been researching and writing a thesis on algorithmic music composition, hence why I worked on "Mathesthesia" for this project - it was a fun blend of my love for math, music, and computer science! After I graduate, I plan on working in the industry as a Software Engineer.

Maddie Bauso

I'm Maddie and I'm a senior about to graduate in May. I'm a Computer Science major and Math and Graphic Design minor. I have a strong interest in the overlap between computer science, art, and math and this influenced our visual emphasis in this WiCHacks project. I am open to a wide variety of avenues that use my various areas of study, especially those involving the web.
